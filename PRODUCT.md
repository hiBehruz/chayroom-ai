# Chayroom AI

## Product Purpose
AI education platform for Uzbek-speaking users. Structured courses, written guides, and a private Telegram community — all in one subscription.

## Users
Young professionals and students in Uzbekistan learning AI tools, no-code, and vibe coding. Mobile-first. They open courses on their phones during breaks or commute.

## Register
product

## Brand
Calm, capable, modern. Not loud. Not techy-corporate. Warm neutrals with a single blue accent (#3480f1). Typography-first layout. Feels like a well-designed notebook.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS (Notion clones, startup landing page templates)
- Dark neon AI aesthetics
- Heavy glassmorphism
- Identical card grids

## Strategic principles
- Trust through clarity: every screen earns the next tap
- Uzbek language, Latin script — copy is warm and direct, never formal bureaucratic
- Subscription drives everything — conversion moments must feel natural, not pushy
